love

I am hopelessly and ridiculously in love with you.
I think I spend my 23 out of 24 thinking about you.
and the very last hour I spend begging myself to fall out of love with you.
And just like quicksand, the more i try to get out of this feeling, the more I fall.
and like an object in air under the influence of gravity, I get a feeling of free fall.

Honestly, if I had a finger for every time II didn't do something for you, I'd have none.
I guess what I am trying to say in essence is that when it comes to you, I am long gone.
And you might not know this but your heart is the bed mine lies upon.
And at this point, I don't need a countdown to tell me that you're the one.

Even more ridiculous is the fact that you're no different.
So stop the petty pretense.
I see how you act all fancy around me like a princess.
I see how your eyes light up when I'm present.
And honestly, if you had a hair plucked out for every time I caught you looking my way, you'd be bald.
For I can tell that your want for me is over and beyond.
I can tell that we prolonging this is a bit over absurd.
I can tell this could be something worth fighting for.
