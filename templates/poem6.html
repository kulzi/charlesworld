My hair and my eye

I’m sorry.
Heartily, sorry.
I probably do owe you an apology.
I lied…I didn’t mean to but I did.
I misled you…I told you I had never been scared and had no fears…but that was a lie.
I’ve always been scared of people knowing who I am…who I truly am.
And I wish I could say, “I don’t give a damn”, but I do…I really do.
My hair…
I’ve always been scared of being an open book.
Because I wasn’t sure if anyone would see me once and dare to have another look.
My eye...
Please don’t...
Don’t look me in the eye...
It’s the path to all souls and my soul
…my soul is an abyss…steadily searching for people to be dragged into this

My eye… And if you think your soul is awash enough for this then go ahead and look at me
Look at me and become perplexed by what you see
Their dark brown not black…my pupils that is
You’d notice if you looked closely
But keep your focus on the right one, I don’t want you to see what’s left of me

My hair…
I got scared so I grew my hair.
My hair and my eye…
And I grew them till they covered my eyes.
And it’s ironic, but I covered my eyes to stop myself from being seen.
I covered my eyes with my hair so no one could tell what I really mean.
So no one could tell where I’m going or where I’ve been.
And so I hid from the world in plain sight.
A star in the morning’s light.
A shadow at night.

And all these I did just so no one knows what I’m about.
Just so no one can see what’s inside and only what’s out.
Just so no one can see through me and turn me inside out.
Just so no one can read the writings on my forehead but only guess
who I am by the words I put on paper or the ones I arranged and shot through my mouth.

Clever isn’t it…I thought to myself.
I have succeeded in deceiving everyone…myself included.
I have excluded myself from the truth and am now secluded
and I have lived my life with intentions that were never intended.
having paid the full mortgage on a life I once rented.
having fallen into the same hole I thought I had eluded.
My hair and my eye…
Why?
Why have you let me live a lie?
