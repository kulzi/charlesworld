part

Our love is much better when we’re apart.
Whenever we are far from each other, that’s when we know how to play our parts.
Perhaps it’s simply a part of who we are or partly because we’re both broken and hoping we’ll heal each other’s scars.

I am furious with myself for tearing you apart.
I promise you that it was never a part of the plan.
The plan was to love you and cherish you till we part ways and even then be able to acknowledge each other at parties.

What does your heart say?
Where does your path lay?
I’m pretty sure it’s on my pathway
Our love which I thought would bring us closer left us divided and angry
Suddenly I couldn’t hear your voice in my driveway
My love for you…had in its way…driven you away

At times I think we live in a movie
So pick up your script and tell me what your part says
Apart from the love and bliss, tell me about the heartbreaks
Did I break you when I decided to part from you?
Did I break you when I told you I didn’t love you?
The truth is…our paths should have never crossed
For the only way this love works is if we love from miles away.
And honestly, I don’t want to take part in a path that's not worth my partake.
I don’t want to wake up in parts and a drumming headache.
I just want to live my life like it says in the script, and you know…do what my part says.
